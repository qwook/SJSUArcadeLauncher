Ideal controls for certain controllers.

-----------------------------------------------------------------
Xbox 360 Controller/Logitech F-310 XInput:
-----------------------------------------------------------------

Under "[Joystick Buttons]" put:
JoyAKeyUp=0
JoyAKeyDown=180
JoyAKeyLeft=270
JoyAKeyRight=90
JoyAKeyA=3
JoyAKeyB=4
JoyAKeyC=2
JoyAKeyD=1
JoyAKeyE=5
JoyAKeyF=6
JoyAStart=8

Change the "A" after Joy to "B" if it is using id 2 of Joystick.


-----------------------------------------------------------------

JoyAKeyUp=0
JoyAKeyDown=180
JoyAKeyLeft=270
JoyAKeyRight=90
JoyAKeyA=1
JoyAKeyB=4
JoyAKeyC=3
JoyAKeyD=2
JoyAKeyE=7
JoyAKeyF=8

Change the "A" after Joy to "B" if it is using id 2 of Joystick.
